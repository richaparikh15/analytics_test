Hawaii Online Continuing Education (CE)

Who will this course appeal to?

This course is perfect for those persons who have a busy schedule but need the freedom to pick and choose their times to study. This course offers those people the needed flexibility in their schedule while still providing a first class learning experience via the internet.

How long does the course last?

Each course varies in length from 2-4 hours. For exact details please look at the specific description of the course(s) you are interested.

What does a course include?

Each course purchased is complete and meets all the necessary requirements to receive credit for respective.

What's the online advantage?

Taking the course online allows you to progress at your own pace as your everyday life dictates. FLEXIBILITY! No need to arrange work or other events around a set class schedule. You have the freedom to take the course when it is convenient for you, a real advantage!. However, there is more! Although you work at your own pace and a schedule that is convenient for you, you still get the professional materials and instruction as in "real" classroom environment. You will be able to email questions to our professional staff and get answers within a 24 hour time period (often sooner). This is the real advantage, all resources as within a real classroom, without losing the personal instruction.
